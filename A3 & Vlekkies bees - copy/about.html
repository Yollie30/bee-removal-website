🐝 Bee Removal Services

We respond to calls from clients who have bee troubles. Once we arrive, we assess the situation and safely vacuum the bees into a specially designed bee box. Our priority is to remove them without harm, ensuring their protection and yours.

🍯 Honey Production

After relocation, the bees are rehabilitated in a safe environment where they can thrive and make honey. When the bees have filled their frames with honey, we collect the honey using a manual drum spinner. The honey is filtered to remove impurities and poured into clean, labeled bottles ready for you to enjoy.

🏪 Selling Honey & Honeycomb

We bottle the fresh, pure honey and package honeycomb for sale. Our products are natural, local, and delicious — perfect for home use or gifts. Keep an eye out for our latest stock and seasonal specials!
